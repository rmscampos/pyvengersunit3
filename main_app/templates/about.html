About Showganize

This app allows you to compile a list of which concerts in your area that you would like to attend. After the concert, you will be able to write reviews and post photos or videos.

Stay tuned for future features that will include an in-app calendar for more streamlined organizing, the ability to connect with your friends and share concert information, and integration with ticket-buying apps.
